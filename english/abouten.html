About Us

Behind the Scene

Our Story

Our bistro was born out of a love for good food, a pleasant atmosphere, and honest work. We believe that food should bring people together, bring joy, and leave an impression — and that’s exactly what we strive for every day. We prepare delicious lunches from fresh ingredients, bake homemade desserts with care, and serve a selection of refreshing lemonades, quality coffee, and seasonal drinks. Whether you come for a quick lunch, a sweet break, or a relaxed gathering with friends, you’ll find a place here where you’ll feel at home.

Our Chef

Behind the flavors of our bistro stands our chef Standa, who puts not only years of experience but also a true passion for honest cuisine into every dish. He believes in simplicity, the quality of ingredients, and an honest approach to cooking — whether it’s a hearty lunch, a fluffy dessert, or a homemade lemonade. His goal is for every guest to feel at home and leave with the feeling that they’ve experienced something more than just good food.
